What is Panchangam?

Panchangam (పంచాంగము) is a Hindu calendar and almanac that follows the traditional units of Hindu timekeeping. The word "Panchangam" comes from the Sanskrit words "Pancha" (five) and "Anga" (limb), referring to the five essential elements of time:

  • Tithi - The lunar day
  • Nakshatra - The constellation or star group
  • Yoga - The auspicious period
  • Karana - Half of a Tithi
  • Var - The day of the week
